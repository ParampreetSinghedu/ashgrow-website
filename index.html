Admissions

How to apply

Ashgrove reads every application in full — no algorithmic first cut.

Submit your application

Complete the Common Application or Ashgrove's own form, along with your transcript and two recommendations.

Aug – Jan

Interview (optional)

Meet with a regional alumni interviewer — helpful, never required, never scored against you.

Nov – Feb

Committee review

Every file is read by at least two admissions officers and discussed by full committee.

Feb – Mar

Decisions released

Admitted students get four weeks to visit campus before committing.
